Strawberry Matcha Latte

Joey Park



A refreshing layered drink with sweet strawberry puree and creamy milk topped with earthy matcha. Perfect for hot days and easy to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh strawberries
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1.5 tsp matcha powder
  • 4 tbsp hot water
  • Ice cubes

Directions

Step 1: Mash strawberries with sugar until it becomes a puree.

Step 2: Add the strawberry puree to a glass and fill with ice.

Step 3: Pour milk over the ice.

Step 4: Mix matcha powder with hot water and whisk until smooth.

Step 5: Pour matcha over the milk to create layers and enjoy.
