Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Total Time: 20 min

Servings: 2 servings

Kimchi Fried Rice

Joey Park

A simple and popular Korean dish made with kimchi and rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 green onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Directions

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and stir fry kimchi for about 1 minute.

Step 2: Add rice, kimchi juice, and gochujang. Mix well.

Step 3: Stir fry everything together for about 5–7 minutes.

Step 4: Add sesame oil and mix.

Step 5: Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds, then serve.
