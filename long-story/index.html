The Tortoise and the Hare

A short story about patience and confidence.

One day...

A hare laughed at a slow tortoise.

The Race

The tortoise said, “Let’s have a race.”

Fast Start

The hare ran very fast and left the tortoise behind.

Too Confident

The hare felt safe, so he stopped and took a nap.

Still Moving

The tortoise kept walking slowly, step by step.

Almost There

The tortoise passed the sleeping hare and reached the finish line.

The End

The tortoise won the race. Slow and steady wins the race.
