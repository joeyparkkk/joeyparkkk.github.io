Prep Time: 25 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Total Time: 35 min

Servings: 10 cookies

Dubai Chewy Cookie

Joey Park

A trendy dessert popular in Korea with a chewy outside and a crispy kadaif pistachio filling. Loved for its mix of chewy texture and crunchy pistachio kadaif filling.

Ingredients

Chewy Dough

  • Marshmallows (150g)
  • Butter (19g)
  • Cocoa powder (13g)
  • Skim milk powder (10g)

Kadaif Pistachio Filling

  • Roasted kadaif noodles (150g)
  • Pistachio paste or spread (150g)

Directions

(If the kadaif noodles are not pre-roasted, toast them in a dry pan over medium heat until golden and crispy. Let them cool.)




Step 1: Make the filling by mixing the roasted kadaif noodles with pistachio paste. Shape into small balls and freeze briefly to firm.

Step 2: Melt marshmallows and butter over low heat.

Step 3: Add sifted cocoa powder and skim milk powder, then mix quickly.

Step 4: Flatten a portion of the melted dough, place the pistachio filling in the center, then wrap the dough around it like a dumpling.

Step 5: Roll the wrapped cookie in cocoa powder to coat the outside, then let the cookies set before serving.
