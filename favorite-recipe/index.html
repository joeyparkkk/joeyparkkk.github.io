Dubai Chewy Cookie

A trendy dessert popular in Korea with a chewy outside and a crispy kadaif pistachio filling. Loved for its mix of chewy texture and crunchy pistachio kadaif filling♥

Ingredients

  • Chewy Dough
    • Marshmallows (150g)
    • Butter (19g)
    • Cocoa powder (13g)
    • Skim milk powder (10g)
  • Kadaif Pistachio Filling
    • Roasted kadaif noodles (150g)
    • Pistachio paste or spread (150g)

Directions

  • (If the kadaif noodles are not pre-roasted, toast them in a dry pan over medium heat until golden and crispy. Let them cool.)
    1. Make the filling by mixing the roasted kadaif noodles with pistachio paste. Shape into small balls and freeze briefly to firm.
    2. Melt marshmallows and butter over low heat.
    3. Add sifted cocoa powder and skim milk powder, then mix quickly.
    4. Flatten a portion of the melted dough and place the pistachio filling balls in the center.
    5. Wrap the dough around the filling like a dumpling.
    6. Roll the wrapped cookie in cocoa powder to coat the outside.
    7. Let the cookies set at room temperature or in the refrigerator before serving.
