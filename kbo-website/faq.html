Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions for new international KBO fans.

Do I need to know all the baseball rules before watching KBO?

No, you do not need to know every rule before watching. It is enough to understand the basic idea of innings, runs, outs, and teams taking turns on offense and defense.

How should I choose a KBO team to follow?

You can choose based on a city, team colors, players, logos, or just what feels interesting to you after watching a few games.

What makes KBO different from MLB?

KBO is known for louder fan culture, organized cheering, and a different playoff system compared to MLB.

Can I watch KBO games outside Korea?

Yes, through YouTube, SOOP, and other platforms depending on your location.

Is KBO beginner-friendly for international fans?

Yes, the active fan culture and simple structure make it easy to get into even without deep knowledge.
