FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions for new international KBO fans.

Do I need to know all the baseball rules before watching KBO?

No, you do not need to know every rule before watching. It is enough to understand the basic idea of innings, runs, outs, and teams taking turns on offense and defense. Many fans learn the details naturally by watching games more often.

How should I choose a KBO team to follow?

There is no perfect way to choose a team. You can pick based on a city, team colors, players, logos, or even the team’s overall fan culture. For new fans, it can also be fun to watch a few games first and see which team feels more interesting to you.

What makes KBO different from MLB?

KBO and MLB both follow the basic rules of baseball, but the game experience can feel different. KBO is known for loud cheering, team songs, organized chants, and a more social stadium atmosphere. The postseason format is also different from MLB.

Can I watch KBO games outside Korea?

Yes, but the options can depend on your location. Some highlights and games are available through YouTube, SOOP, and other streaming platforms. Some content may also be region-restricted, so access can change depending on where you are watching from.

Is KBO beginner-friendly for international fans?

Yes, KBO can be beginner-friendly because the fan culture is very active and fun to follow. Even if you do not understand every rule at first, the teams, cheering style, and game atmosphere can make it easier to get interested. This site is meant to help new fans start with simple information.
