About

Learn more about the purpose of this website and why it was created.

Why This Site Exists

KBO Fans Worldwide is designed for international fans who want to follow Korean baseball more easily. Many KBO resources are difficult to find or not available in English, so this site brings useful information into one place.

This website focuses on helping new and existing fans understand teams, find ways to watch games, and explore the KBO community.

Who This Site Is For

This site is mainly for international fans who are interested in KBO but do not know where to start. It is also helpful for people who are familiar with baseball but want to learn more about Korean baseball.
